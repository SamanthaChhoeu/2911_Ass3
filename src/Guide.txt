Warehouse Boss
Welcome to Warehouse Boss Game!
Please enter your game first and try to get all boxes sorted!

Here are some basic operations:
↑: Move ahead
↓: Move to back
←: Move to left
→: Move to right

And when you press:
U: Undo to last step
R: Restart the game

Save your work by simply press "Save" button while playing;
Turn on or off BGM by pressing "Set Sound" button in game window, or in settings.

That is all, enjoy the game!
